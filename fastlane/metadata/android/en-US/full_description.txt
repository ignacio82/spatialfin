SpatialFin is a Jellyfin client built for Android XR and adapted for phones, tablets, TV, and other Android form factors.

Enjoy an immersive theater experience on Android XR with Home Space and Full Space playback modes, or use SpatialFin as a native Jellyfin client on the rest of your Android devices.

Features:
- Immersive Android XR playback with spatial theater controls
- Native Jellyfin browsing for movies, shows, seasons, and episodes
- ASS/SSA anime subtitle support, subtitle search, and track selection
- Downloads and offline playback
- Local media and network share playback
- Companion setup and cross-device playback features

You need your own Jellyfin server to use most app features.

SpatialFin is a third-party Jellyfin client and is not affiliated with the Jellyfin project.
